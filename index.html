About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the School of Political Science & Public Administration, Wuhan University. I received my PhD in International Law from Wuhan University (2024) and LL.B. from Sun Yat-sen University (2019). My research focuses on international economic law, with particular interests in international trade and investment policy, investor-State dispute settlement (ISDS), and subsidy regulation in international economic law.
